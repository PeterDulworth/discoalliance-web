Disco Texas | 2587

Based out of Houston, TX; The members of this chapter were the founding members of the Discobots Alliance. They have helped structure the Discobots Alliance to the point at which it is now. 2587 is considered one of the most powerful teams in the VEX Robotics Competition ecosystem, due to their immense presence and outstanding performance at world championships.

Disco Canada | 1104

As the second chapter of the Discobots Alliance, 1104/1140 has rapidly risen to fame in just 2 years, by holding 2 World Champion titles, 2 World Finalists titles, and 10 Top 30 Robot Skills titles.

Disco California | 523

Discobots California was founded after the 2015-2016 season, and sought to improve robotics education in the Greater Los Angeles area. Their members have participated in VEX previously under team numbers 62 and 1104H.

Disco Galveston | 400

Just one hour away from our founding team, Team 400 has made an immense impact upon many VEX teams. They too hold a World Champion title, a National Champion title, in addition to many regional awards. 400s is the only team to have won the state, national and world titles in the same season.
