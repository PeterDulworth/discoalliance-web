Disco Texas | 2587

View Team
Based out of Houston, TX, team 2587 was the first member of the Discobots Alliance. Members come from various schools in Houston including Lamar and Homeschool. The Texas DiscoBots were founded in December 2007, and since then, have worked to bring together students, teachers, and mentors who all love technology with the goals of designing, building, and programing robots for the VEX robotics competition, spreading awareness to their community about robotics, and encouraging students to pursue careers in STEM.

The second chapter of the Discobots Alliance (1104/1140), Brampton Robotics was founded in 2014. Disco Canada holds two VEX Robotics World Champion titles, two VEX Robotics World Finalists titles, and ten top thirty Robot Skills titles.

Discobots California was founded after the 2015-2016 season. Their members have participated in VEX previously under team numbers 62 (Skyrise) and 1104H (Nothing but Net). Now they run 523 (VRC) and 532 (IQ) with 4 teams on their roster.

Team 400 has made an immense impact upon many VEX teams. 400 holds a VEX Robotics World Champion title, two National Champion titles, and two Texas State Championship titles. 400s is the only team to have won the state, national and world titles in a single season (Nothing but Net).
